Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Project Data

All project data you create in the Q-SYS Plugin Layout Editor is stored locally on your computer using your browser's local storage. Your projects, settings, and layout data are never transmitted to or stored on any external server.

Analytics and Visitor Tracking

We may collect anonymous, aggregate information about visits to this site, including:

  • Number of unique visitors
  • Pages visited
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Referring website
  • Date and time of visit

This information is used solely to understand how the site is being used and to improve the editor. It does not include any personally identifiable information or any data from your projects.

Cookies and Local Storage

This site uses browser local storage to save your editor settings, project data, and preferences (such as the disclaimer acknowledgement). No tracking cookies are used by the editor itself. If analytics are implemented, they may use cookies to identify unique visitors.

Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer any information to third parties.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
